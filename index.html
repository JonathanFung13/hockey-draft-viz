NHL Draft

Visualization Overview

This visualization displays the results of the last 15 NHL drafts. The teams are displayed alphabetically within each division. The draft picks are represented by a circle. Each circle is labeled with the position of the player and the color corresponds to the legend at the top. The legend can be toggled through three modes: Roster Status, Games Played per Season and Career Points per Game.
  • Use the radio buttons to change legend modes
  • Selecting a team's draft history displays a detailed view of the team's history below
  • Click a circle in the legend to toggle that color on or off in the visualization
  • For basic player information, mouse-over the circles in the detailed draft view.
  • To see complete information about a player, click on the player circle in the detailed draft view
  • For more information about the three modes, hover over the names of the modes.
